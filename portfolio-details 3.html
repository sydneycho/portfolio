T-shirt Rebranding

Project information

  • Category: Web design
  • Client: SS Innovations
  • Project date: 01 March, 2020
  • Project URL: coming soon

The t-shirt branding project aimed to create a unique and eye-catching design for a clothing brand targeting young adults. The design concept was inspired by street art and urban culture, incorporating bold typography, graphic elements, and vibrant colors. The team conducted extensive research to ensure that the design resonated with the target audience and aligned with the brand's values. The final design was well-received by the client and successfully implemented across a range of t-shirts, helping to establish the brand's identity and appeal to its desired market.
